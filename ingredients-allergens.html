What's in every bar

Exactly what the soap is made of, in plain language, plus an honest word about lye and essential-oil sensitivities. No medical claims, no fine print.

One simple base, five scents

Every bar is made from the same base: coconut oil and shea butter, saponified with lye (sodium hydroxide), then scented with essential oils. That's the whole recipe, only the essential oils change from scent to scent.

Lye does the work that turns oils into soap. During saponification it reacts completely with the coconut oil and shea butter, and once the bars are cured, no active lye remains in the finished soap. That's how all traditional handmade bar soap is made.

A word on allergens

Every bar is scented with essential oils, and essential oils are a common sensitivity for some people. If you have a known fragrance or essential-oil allergy, reach out to Jelisha before you order and she'll talk you through the scents so you can pick one that works for you.
